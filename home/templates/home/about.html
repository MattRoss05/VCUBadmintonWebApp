What is VCU Badminton about?

🏸 Competitive Play

The VCU Badminton Club brings together students who love the game—whether you're aiming for intense competition or just learning.

Looking to sharpen your skills and play competitively? We’ve got you. From casual games to traveling for tournaments, the VCU Badminton Club gives you a chance to grow your game and be part of a team that values both focus and friendship.

Our club is all about improving together, competing with confidence, and enjoying every game along the way. Come for the challenge, stay for the community.
